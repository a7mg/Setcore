Local Programs Contribution

Setcore is one of the early participants and adopters of local content programs such as IKTVA in Saudi Arabia and ICV in the UAE. In Saudi Arabia, we hold the IKTVA certificate from Saudi Aramco, emphasizing our role in local economic growth, job creation, and sustainability. This certification enhances our competitiveness by ensuring a significant local content contribution. Similarly, in the UAE, Setcore holds the ICV certificate, furthering our commitment to the local economy through job creation and sustainable practices, while maintaining a strong competitive position in the market.

Globally Recognized Certifications

With a series of globally accredited certifications, we are the partner to trust with all oil and gas inspection endeavors.

We hold prestigious ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001 certifications and uphold globally recognized Standard Operating Procedures for both Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) and Quality Management Systems.

Additionally, our commitment to excellence is evident in our adherence to international industry standards, including API, BV (T H Hill), DS 1 NS, BS, ASTM, LEEA, DROPS, and ABS, ensuring that our Inspection Services consistently meet the highest benchmarks of quality and safety.
