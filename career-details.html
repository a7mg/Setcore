Operations Engineer

Operations Engineer (Process & Planning) (Direct Contract - Fixed Term Employee)

Cairo, Egypt

Apply
Role Purpose

Plan, develop, and provide day to day process engineering surveillance of offshore and offshore tie in facilities to assure process stability to deliver gas and condensate to onshore facilities as per nomination.

Plan, schedule, and monitor general operations activities by close communication with the offshore team, onshore support team, relevant departments and external stake holders to ensure all activities are managed and executed in a safe, professional and timely manner.

Responsibilities include but not limited
  • Develop and implement process engineering surveillance plans for offshore and offshore tie in facilities to ensure
  • Provide process engineering deliverables for day-to-day operation of offshore and offshore tie in gas facilities, including process troubleshooting, root cause analysis, plant performance test, and process calculation.
  • Proactively seeking plant improvement by performing plant performance analysis, plant optimization, and feasibility study on plant modification.
  • Provide detail process engineering support (basic design narrative and calculation, PFD, P&ID) and lead HAZOP as needed for project activity.
  • Act as focal point for POPM (Plant Operating Procedures Manual) review and modification.
  • Lead, establish and update the operations integrated plan including coordination with relevant department, external parties/stakeholder in a timely manner to ensure safe, effective, smooth and reliable operations activities.
  • Manage, coordinate, and monitor shutdown work with maintenance planner, offshore team, and related parties to ensure all required tasks are completed safely and in accordance with related schedule.
  • As the single point of contact for offshore tie in facilities; manage and monitor offshore tie in performance to ensure safe and reliable deliverables of offshore tie in facilities.
  • Coordination with partners, vendors, government (SKK Migas) and internal business unit team to ensure smooth operations of offshore tie in facilities.
  • Maintain and update monthly KPI data by coordinating and liaising with relevant team member department to ensure all data is valid prior to dissemination.
  • Prepare and coordinate WP&B process and monitoring for offshore and offshore tie in facilities, including but not limited to coordination with related stakeholder (SKK Migas, Partners, etc).
  • Produce regular offshore and offshore tie in facilities cost report to capture and monitor the expenditure that will be used for internal and/or external (JV, SKK Migas).
  • Maintain offshore and offshore tie in operations owners cost and update it accordingly with actual cost.
  • Analyze monthly departmental budget expenditure.
  • Ensure compliance to all relevant safety, quality and environmental policies, procedures and controls within sphere of influence (falling under the Operating Framework) to effectively ensure employee safety, corporate and legislative compliance whilst delivering daily contract gas quantity.
  • Plan and promote all work to be implemented in a safe manner in support of Companyâ€™s HSSE objectives.
  • Shall comply with all Company HSSE Policy, Standards, and Procedures.
