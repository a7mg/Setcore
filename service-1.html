Inspection Services

At Setcore, we prioritize the safety and reliability of our clients’ operations, which is why our team of experts utilize cutting-edge technologies and industry best practices to conduct thorough inspections.

OCTG
Inspection Services

Our OCTG inspections adhere to the highest standards required by the oil and gas industry, ensuring both operational efficiency and safety.

Drill Pipe We offer thorough inspection services for drill pipes to guarantee their structural integrity and reliability during drilling operations.
HWDP, Drill Collars and Sub We conduct detailed inspections of heavy-weight drill pipes (HWDP), drill collars, and subs to identify any flaws or abnormalities that could potentially compromise drilling operations.
Tubing, Casing and Sucker Rod Our inspections cover tubing, casing, and sucker rods, aiming to detect defects, corrosion, or any other issues that could affect their performance in oil and gas wells.
Motors, Jars, and Other Downhole Tools At Setcore, we complete inspection services for downhole tools like motors, jars, and other equipment, ensuring their functionality and safety in downhole environments.
Pipe Tally, condition monitoring and pipe management. We provide services dedicated to pipe inventory management, condition monitoring, and tracking. Our goal is to optimize inventory levels and guaranteethe availability of high-quality pipes for all drilling operations.

Rig Inspection Services

Our surface rig inspection services carefully evaluate the structures and equipment of drilling rigs used in well construction, ensuring that the drilling rigs are fully equipped to complete flawless well creation.

Complete Rig Inspections

Our comprehensive inspections cover all components and systems of drilling rigs to ensure they meet safety standards and optimal operations.
