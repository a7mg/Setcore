Through our present principal partnerships, we provide innovative and unique technologies to the oil and gas industry in our areas of operation. Our solutions ensure the safe and efficient operation of wells, facilitate well intervention, optimize production, secure plug and abandonment (P&A) operations, maintain well integrity, reach lateral sections in horizontal wells, conduct effective pipe recovery operations, and deploy advanced completion tools and multi-stage cementing solutions.

Currently, we are proud to partner with globally leading technology providers such as Archer®, Welltec®, and MCR®. These companies specialize in well intervention, integrity, plug and abandonment and pipe recovery. By collaborating with us, they are able to introduce their solutions and tools to the Egyptian market without the need for costly operating base and standalone legal entities.
