Plug and Abandonment

At Setcore, we provide comprehensive Plug and Abandonment (P&A) solutions, ensuring efficiency, safety, and regulatory compliance. Our services cover everything from custom plug options to full P&A and slot recovery operations, decommissioning your wells smoothly and cost-effectively.

Plug Solutions

Our plug systems offer significant time and cost savings across applications like well integrity, suspension, and abandonment. Key features include:

  • Wide range of sizes for various well configurations
  • Pipe or E-line deployment options
  • V-0 rated plugs for tight gas wells
  • Dual Plug System for setting two plugs in a single run

P&A and Slot Recovery

Our P&A and slot recovery services safely decommission wells and recover valuable slots. Services include:

  • Casing cut and pull
  • Casing exit with whipstocks
  • Single and dual section milling
  • Perforate, wash, and cement

At Setcore, we use advanced technology and industry expertise to deliver reliable, compliant, and environmentally responsible P&A solutions.
